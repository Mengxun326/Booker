This CBZ archive contains ten photos, found on the internet, of part of the city of Avignon, in the south of France.

It's a magnificent city, isn't it?

Here is the link to the Wikipedia page: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Avignon

In particular, you can see the Palais des Papes, for which here is the Wikipedia page: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palais_des_Papes

You can also see the Saint-Bénézet Bridge, also known as the "Pont d'Avignon". It is a place mentioned in a famous children's nursery rhyme, "Sur le Pont d'Avignon".

Here are the lyrics:

Sur le pont d'Avignon
